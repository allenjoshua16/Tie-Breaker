# The Tiebreaker

AI-Powered Strategic Decision Assistant

---

## Overview

The Tiebreaker is an AI-powered decision support application designed to reduce decision fatigue and bring structured clarity to complex choices.

Instead of relying on simple pros-and-cons lists, the platform acts as a strategic digital sounding board that evaluates options through logic, weighted reasoning, contextual analysis, and AI-assisted insights.

Whether the decision involves career moves, business strategies, investments, personal dilemmas, or long-term planning, The Tiebreaker transforms uncertainty into organized and explainable recommendations.

---

## Core Features

- AI-assisted decision analysis
- Multi-factor comparison system
- Weighted scoring and prioritization
- Structured pros and cons generation
- Bias reduction through objective reasoning
- Interactive and responsive UI
- Real-time strategic insights
- Clean visualization of decision outcomes
- Fast and scalable frontend experience
- Cloud deployment support
